Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between a Master and Mechanical license?

A Mechanical License allows you to record your own version of our song and sell copies of it. A Master Use License gives you permission to use our actual studio recording (the master track) for your own project or performance.

How long does the licensing process take?

Once you submit your initial request through our catalog, a publishing coordinator will review your parameters and follow up within 24-48 business hours with official documentation and rate structures.

Can I license a song not listed in the catalog?

Yes! If you know of a track in our broader catalog or need a custom arrangement, simply type the name directly into the song search bar on Step 2 of the Licensing Form.
